About Me

I am a third-year PhD student at the EPSRC CDT in Industrially Focused Mathematical Modelling, Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford. I am supervised by Prof. Ben Hambly. My research interests lie in reinforcement learning, stochastic control, game theory, and mathematical finance.
