About me

I am a third-year PhD student at the EPSRC CDT in Industrially Focused Mathematical Modelling, Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford. I am supervised by Prof. Ben Hambly. Previously, I completed my 2+2 (dual) undergraduate degree at University of Manchester and Shandong University in 2018.

Research Interests

Reinforcement learning, stochastic control, game theory, and mathematical finance.

Publications

Ben Hambly, Renyuan Xu, and Huining Yang. Policy Gradient Methods for the Noisy Linear Quadratic Regulator over a Finite Horizon. Accepted, SIAM Journal of Control and Optimization, 2021.
